1. Introduction

Deniz Maral ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") operates the MarAI Keyboard application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

To be absolutely clear, MarAI Keyboard does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Any text you type using the keyboard
  • Passwords, credit card numbers, or any sensitive information
  • Your contacts, messages, or communication content
  • Your location data
  • Your browsing history or app usage patterns
  • Personal identifiers or user profiles
  • Any data to external servers

3. Local Data Storage

The App stores certain preferences and settings locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and includes:

3.1 User Preferences

  • Keyboard theme and appearance settings
  • Language preferences and enabled languages
  • Keyboard layout preferences
  • Sound and haptic feedback settings
  • Favorite emojis and emoji skin tone preferences

3.2 Usage Data (Device-Only)

  • Recently used emojis (stored locally for quick access)
  • Auto-correction and suggestion preferences
All this data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and App Group containers). We have no access to this data.

4. No Network Access Required

MarAI Keyboard operates entirely offline. The keyboard extension does not request or require "Full Access" that would enable network connectivity. All features work without an internet connection:

  • Word suggestions use Apple's built-in UITextChecker (on-device)
  • Language detection uses Apple's Natural Language framework (on-device)
  • All processing happens locally on your device

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple App Store

If you make an in-app purchase (Pro version), the transaction is processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information. Apple's Privacy Policy governs their handling of your payment data.

5.2 TestFlight (Beta Testing)

If you use the App through Apple's TestFlight program, Apple may collect diagnostic and usage information according to their TestFlight Privacy Policy. This is managed by Apple, not by us.

5.3 Third-Party Libraries

The App uses open-source libraries (such as EmojiKit) that operate entirely on-device and do not collect or transmit any user data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect any data, the App is safe for users of all ages. Parents can feel confident that their children's typing and communications remain private.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your data on any servers, there is no risk of data breaches affecting your information through our App. Local data stored on your device is protected by iOS's built-in security features, including:

  • Device encryption
  • App sandboxing
  • Secure keychain storage

8. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Settings: You can reset all App preferences through the App's settings
  • Uninstall: Deleting the App removes all locally stored data
  • No Account Required: There is no account to delete because we never create one

9. International Users

The App is available internationally. Since we do not collect or transfer any personal data, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. Your data stays on your device regardless of your location.

9.1 GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area: Since we do not collect any personal data, GDPR data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, etc.) are automatically satisfied. There is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete because we never collect it in the first place.

9.2 CCPA Compliance (California Users)

For California residents: We do not sell personal information because we do not collect any. The App complies with CCPA by design through our zero-data-collection approach.
